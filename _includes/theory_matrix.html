Comparison of International Relations Theories
Theory Primary Actors Human Nature International System Primary Focus
Realism States Selfish / Flawed Anarchic, zero-sum game Power, Security, Survival
Liberalism States, IOs, NGOs, Corporations Basically good, capable of cooperation Anarchic but mitigated by institutions Cooperation, Interdependence, Peace
Constructivism Individuals, NGOs, States Socially constructed Anarchy is what states make of it Ideas, Identities, Norms
Marxism Classes (Capitalist vs Proletariat) Shaped by economic conditions Hierarchical (Core, Periphery) Economic inequality, Exploitation
