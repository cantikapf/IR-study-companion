Key Historical Events in IR
1648
Peace of Westphalia
Established the concept of state sovereignty, laying the foundation for the modern state system.
1815
Congress of Vienna
Created the Concert of Europe, an early attempt to maintain balance of power and prevent major wars.
1914 - 1918
World War I
The collapse of empires and the subsequent creation of the League of Nations, driven by Wilsonian idealism.
1939 - 1945
World War II
Ended with the creation of the United Nations and the dawn of the nuclear age, shifting the global order.
1947 - 1991
The Cold War
Bipolar world order dominated by the US and USSR, characterized by ideological struggle and proxy wars.
2001
September 11 Attacks
Shifted global focus to non-state actors, terrorism, and marked the beginning of the "War on Terror".
